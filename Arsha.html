TRADING

Arise Trading & Maintenance W.L.L company based in Kingdom of Bahrain, is a dynamic company specialized in diverse range of products and services. The company is engaged in trading of premium quality Thermal paper rolls, Thermal paper labels, and a variety of other paper products. In addition, we supplies hotels with essential hygiene items and offers an array of unique corporate gifts.

Services

MAINTENANCE

Alongside its trading activities, Arise Trading & Maintenance undertakes comprehensive maintenance projects such us General Maintenance, Interior Works and Fitout Projects, Exterior Works, Water Proofing, Electrical, Plumbing Works, Gardening and Stone Paving Works etc. positioning itself as a reliable partner for businesses looking for both quality products and service excellence.
